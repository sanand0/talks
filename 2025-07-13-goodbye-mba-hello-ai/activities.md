# Activities

1. **Benchmark LLM arithmetic on multiplication tasks**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: before the talk
  - Model/tool: LLaMA 2, LLaMA 3, GPT-4.1 Mini, Gemini 2.0 Flash, GPT 3.5 Turbo
  - What happened: Anand compared several models on integer multiplication to show where they fail.
  - Source:
    > `If you ask an LLM to multiply two numbers, and I did`
    > `When asked for the two-digit multiplication, it did not get it right at all.`

2. **Cross-check multiple models to reduce classification errors**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: before the talk
  - Model/tool: GPT-4.1 Mini, LLaMA 3, Gemini 2.5 Flash Preview, Claude 3.5 Haiku
  - What happened: Anand compared model disagreements and used cross-checking to cut the error rate.
  - Source:
    > `If the models are in disagreement, let's do a cross-check.`
    > `using 2-5 diverse models cut classification errors from 14 % to < 1 %`

3. **Use LLMs as study sparring partners and rehearsal tools**
  - Who: audience
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: ChatGPT, Custom GPTs, LLMs
  - What happened: Anand told students to upload course material, quiz themselves, rehearse cases, and keep a prompt library.
  - Source:
    > `Use LLMs to make _you_ smarter`
    > `Have ChatGPT quiz you like a CFO, COO, regulator`

4. **Tell the LLM to do the assignments and offload grunt work**
  - Who: audience
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: LLMs
  - What happened: Anand explicitly encouraged students to delegate routine coursework and paperwork to LLMs.
  - Source:
    > `Please sleep. Tell the LLM to do the assignments`
    > `Don't memorize or do the grunt work. Delegate that to LLMs.`
