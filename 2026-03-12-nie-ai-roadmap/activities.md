# Activities

1. **Use ChatGPT and Gemini to create a logistics slide deck from current events**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: ChatGPT, Gemini
  - What happened: Anand dictated a classroom logistics prompt into ChatGPT, then pasted it into Gemini to generate slides.
  - Source:
    > `I'm going to open ChatGPT and dictate.`
    >
    > `I'm going to paste this into Gemini.`

2. **Use AI to find factual errors in an NCERT history textbook**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: ChatGPT, Gemini, or similar LLMs
  - What happened: Anand's intern fed the textbook to an AI model and found factual and precision errors.
  - Source:
    > `take ChatGPT, Gemini, something or the other, feed it a textbook. Have it go page by page and find a mistake in the textbook.`
    >
    > `it identified 45 claims in the textbook and said there is one factual error, two precision errors, and two questionable claims.`
