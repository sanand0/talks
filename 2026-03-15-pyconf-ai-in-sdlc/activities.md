# Activities

1. **Record a call, transcribe it with Gemini, and turn it into code with Copilot/Codex/Claude**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: Gemini, Copilot, Codex, Claude, ChatGPT
  - What happened: Anand described a full SDLC pipeline where one model transcribes, another writes code, and another tests and deploys.
  - Source:
    > `I record the call. Then I send it to Gemini and say, "Tell me what he wants."`
    >
    > `Convert that into a prompt for Copilot or Codex or Claude. Give it to it, it generates the code.`

2. **Use agents to test, deploy, and email results automatically**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: AI agents
  - What happened: Anand had agents find errors, deploy the code, and send a completion email.
  - Source:
    > `Then I have one of the other agents say, "Look, test it, find all the errors that you can," feed it back`
    >
    > `And automatically send an email to these people with appropriate content saying this particular transcript has been deployed.`

3. **Use ChatGPT as a transcription and prompt engine for interns**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: ChatGPT
  - What happened: Anand said his interns record calls, feed them to ChatGPT, and get the result.
  - Source:
    > `he records the call, he feeds it to ChatGPT, and gets the result.`

4. **Use ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude as delegated reviewers for prompt improvement**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude
  - What happened: Anand and the panel described using one model to improve prompts and another to review the response.
  - Source:
    > `I make ChatGPT come up with a summary.`
    >
    > `I would want, say, Gemini or DeepSeek to validate or review your response`

5. **Use Claude and Gemini to generate code and UI for rapid prototypes**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: Claude, Gemini, Copilot, Codex
  - What happened: The panel described using LLMs for coding assistance, regression testing, and rapid prototyping.
  - Source:
    > `using LLMs for coding assistance, automating regression testing`
    >
    > `Claude Code is just amazing.`

6. **Have agents cross-check PRs, skills, and workflow preferences**
  - Who: speaker
  - When: during the talk
  - Model/tool: AI agents
  - What happened: Anand described capturing his preferences in transcripts and having an agent turn them into a `skills.md`.
  - Source:
    > `having some agent look at all of this and say, "What are all the things that I've said more than thrice?"`
    >
    > `Take that and put it into a skills.md.`
